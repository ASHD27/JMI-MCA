You can use SWI prolog interpreter freely available. Write your program in notepad and save it with extension .pl

Open SWI prolog, then from Menu, click consult to browse your program say water.pl

At command prompt pose a query, to solve puzzle as shown below:
    ?- solve((0,0), (2,_).

In this prolog query, we are providing initial state, (0,0) i.e. both jug empty, goal state (2,_), in prolog underscore indicates
 don't care value of this variable.
